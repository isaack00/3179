Global Disasters & Australian Bushfires

An interactive dashboard exploring trends and impacts from global and local datasets.

Australian Fire Hotspots

This map visualizes fire hotspots across Australia. The size and color of each circle represent the brightness temperature (in Kelvin) detected by satellites, which is a proxy for fire intensity.

Annual Disaster Distribution by Country

This box plot compares the distribution of the number of disasters that occur each year for several key countries, providing insight into the variability and consistency of disaster frequency.

Global Disaster Frequency by Country

This map shows the frequency of natural disasters across the globe for a selected year. Countries with a higher frequency are shaded in a darker red. Use the slider to explore how disaster frequency has changed over time. Countries with no recorded data for the selected year are shown in grey.
